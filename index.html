#bball4ever
basketball is awesome! come play with us!!
hey! we're a non-profit team based at Montano College, a school in Guatemala. we wanna get all grades playing basketball, so we host tournaments against other schools to up our game (and because it's just fun).

everyone should get a shot at playing, no matter your level.

basketball is a great sport!

you don't need to be tall, fast, or super skilled. the only thing you need is a ball, a hoop (or a trash can), and the courage to shoot your shot!
